# 🎃 31 Nights of Horror

A Halloween advent calendar for October 2026. Open a pumpkin each night to reveal a horror movie to watch — one per day, all month long.

---

## How It Works

Every day in October, click that day's pumpkin on the calendar. A hand reaches in, lifts the lid off the jack-o-lantern, and pulls out a slip of paper with the night's horror movie pick. Once you've opened a day, the pumpkin stays lit and the movie title is revealed on the calendar.

Your progress is saved automatically in your browser, so you can come back each night without losing track of what you've already opened.

---

## Features

- 🗓️ Full October 2026 calendar grid (Sun–Sat)
- 🎃 31 hand-picked horror films, one per night
- 👻 Jack-o-lantern animation with hand, lid, and paper slip reveal
- 🌕 Spooky atmosphere — night sky, moon, flickering pumpkin eyes
- 💾 Progress saved per browser via localStorage
- 📱 Responsive — works on mobile and desktop
